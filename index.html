Personal Information

I am an Australian citizen living and working in North Queensland. The highest education I have completed is Grade 12, however I am currently working towards my Bachelor of Information Technology. For the past 2 years I have been living and working in London, which was an incredible experience and opened my eyes to the exciting world of travel. Aside from travel, I enjoy rollerblading with my young nieces and nephews and spending time with my family in general.

Interest in IT

What is your interest in IT?

My interest in IT stems from a young age, as I always appreciated using the computer in school. I can recall starting out for the first time learning how to touch type, using the ‘Mario Touch Typing’ program. At the time I thought it was so much fun (I was also very competitive) so I quickly excelled. Little did I know that this was just the tip of the iceberg when it came to the world of computers. My first computer studies teacher was Mr. Hobson, coincidently his wife was also my maths teacher. I did not like Maths at the time, however I remember this changing by the end of the year. It is funny how certain people have a way in which they can teach you to find an appreciation for subjects that you didn’t think you could. Mr Hobson provided me with a great introduction which peaked my interest, this was evident in my career, although finance based, my position always incorporated a strong amount of IT interaction.

Why did you choose to come to RMIT?

After hours of back and forth between degrees and career objectives, I finally landed on my Bachelor of Information Technology. I believe that by completing this degree, I will provide myself with the greatest opportunity to progress and succeed in my career. As I am still working full time whilst studying, RMIT offered the option of completing the course subjects on a personal timeline, without having to enrol and be accepted, which suited my needs.

What do you expect to learn during your studies?

Upon completion of my studies, I hope that I have fulfilled the requirements of the course and leave with my Bachelor of Information Technology. Although receiving my Bachelor’s degree is the ultimate goal, I do hope that my understanding of all material is sound and I can confidently move into the IT sector in a professional capacity.
